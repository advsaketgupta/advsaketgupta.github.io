Our Practices Areas

Taxation law

The only thing that can hurts you more than paying any income tax/GST/TDS etc is not having enough money to pay taxes.


Civil law

Laws and principles are not for the times when there is no temptation: they are for such moments as this, when body and soul rise in mutiny against their rigour ... If at my convenience I might break them, what would be their worth?

Property Registry

The law of registration of documents is contained in the Indian Registration Act, 1908. This law provides for the registration of various documents, to ensure conservation of evidence, prevention of fraud and assurance of title.

TIR

A clear title is conclusive proof of ownership. So whenever a real estate transaction takes place a thorough title search is done to ensure clear and marketable title because a defective title may land you in legal and financial trouble.

Cheque Bounce

It is easy to write a cheque but one must take care of a few things before issuing it to someone else as the error might cause your cheque to bounce.



Family matters

A family lawyer can act as an objective third party to a couple that is separating. ... They can make sure each person involved in a divorce gets what they need to move on with their life. It is always a good idea to hire an attorney in cases of divorce, child custody and domestic abuse
